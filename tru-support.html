How does Tru-Support Work?

CAPTIVATE THE CUSTOMER

We take a customer who wants to cancel and turn them into a listener. We accomplish this by directing them to the customer cancellation portal in order to expedite their cancellation. Once there the customer must watch the 5-minute video presentation before canceling.

EDUCATE THE CUSTOMER

Now that we have the customers attention we begin by addressing some common misconceptions in regards to canceling Vehicle Service Contracts, Gap, Maintenance, and other cancellable products. At the same time, we are able to dispell the belief that by canceling the products their payments will go down.

CREATE REVENUE

We take a negative and turn it into a positive revenue for the dealership. We do this by charging a $75 fee for the expedited processing of their cancellation. We also eliminate costly errors in time and mileage discrepancies at the same time. The dealer has now taken what was a lost revenue scenario and turned it around.

GETTING STARTED IS AS EASY AS 1-2-3

Step 1 - Intitial Consultaion:

We begin with a free consultation with the Owner or GM of the dealership. We explain the four main areas of recovery and how inaccurate chargebacks may be affecting the dealership. We give an estimate of what we believe might be recoverable to the dealer. Only after the dealer is comfortable then we move on to contracting.

Step 2 - Tentative Agreement:

Our contract is a simple eight-point agreement that gets right to the bottom line. It addresses the scope of the work, its limitations and how we are only paid on monies that are actually recovered. A Non Disclosure Agreement is required to protect the dealer and the processes UDS uses in recovery.

Step 3 - Process:

Our processes include Real-Time processing and Historical Processing. We begin with the Real-Time processing and move into the Historical Process after we prove our model works. Our Historical Process can recover chargeback errors up to the maximum limitations set by the state.

PRIVACY AND SECURITY

Dealers understandably don’t want to expose their records to third parties. Fortunately, we don’t require DMS or CRM access to complete our work. Our staff works directly from chargeback records, so there’s never any need to access private information.

Face it; confidential data is crucial to keep your business running. We never overstep our bounds and always work within the scope of the project.
