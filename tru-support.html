GETTING STARTED IS AS EASY AS 1-2-3

Step 1 - Intitial Consultaion:

In our initial consultation, usually with the owner or GM of the dealership, we explain the four main areas of recovery and how inaccurate chargebacks affect the dealership. We then give an estimate of what we believe might be recoverable to the dealer. Finally, and only after the dealer is comfortable with our plan, we move on to the contract.

Step 2 - Tentative Agreement:

Our contract is a simple eight-point agreement that gets right to the bottom line. It addresses the scope of the work, its limitations, and how we are only paid on monies that are recovered. A Non-Disclosure Agreement is required to protect the dealer and the processes UDS uses in recovery.

Step 3 - Process:

Our processes include Real-Time processing and Historical Processing. We begin with the Real-Time processing and move into the Historical Processing once we prove our model works. Our Historical Processing can recover chargeback errors up to the maximum limitations set by the state.

PRIVACY AND SECURITY

Dealers don’t want to expose their records to third parties. That’s understandable. Fortunately, we don’t require DMS or CRM access to complete our work. Instead, our staff works directly from chargeback records, so there is never any need to access private information. We know confidential data is crucial to keeping your business running, so we ensure to stay within the scope of the project and never to overstep our remit.
