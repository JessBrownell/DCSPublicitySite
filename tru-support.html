CUSTOMER RETENTION

Once a customer is directed to your customized Tru-Support system, we immediately address the misconceptions they may have about cancelling their F&I products. When a customer cancels their F&I product, it directly affects your bottom line. Well not anymore! Our system offers your customers choices. Most of us aren’t aware that up to 20% of customer cancellations contain errors which cost your Dealership thousands of dollars. Our software removes any chance of these errors, so you can rest assured that you are saving thousands you might have otherwise lost.
