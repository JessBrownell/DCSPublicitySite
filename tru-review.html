What is Tru-Review?

ABOUT

TRU-REVIEW starts immediately after the first 60 days. DCS will have 60 days of TRU-TIME data, along with 60 days of your dealership’s total savings. We will also have 60 days where the lenders are cancelling the way they’re supposed to, rather than the way they want to. This will set the precedent needed to recover up to 10 years’ worth of overpayments that your Dealership could be owed!


Lenders have requested dealers to cancel incorrectly for decades. From day one challenging these lenders we’ve noticed one consistency; we’ve never witnessed a lender cancellation that was in the dealer’s favour. What does this tell you?


With the data we collect, our experts will gather a TRU-REVIEW that is tailored to your dealership. We can then refer you to our legal resources and assist you with collecting any monies due from the past.
