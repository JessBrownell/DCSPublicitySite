What is Tru-Review?

ABOUT

Tru-Review starts immediately after the first 60 days. DCS will have 60 days of Tru-Time data, along with 60 days of your Dealerships total savings. We will also have 60 days of the lenders cancelling correctly the way they are supposed to, not the way they feel is correct. This will set the precedence needed to recover your overpayments for up to 10 years back.


Lenders have requested Dealers to cancel incorrectly for decades. Do you think their inaccurate cancellation equation benefits them or you? From day one of us challenging Lenders, we’ve noticed one consistency; never have we witnessed a lender cancellation that has ever been in the Dealers favor. What does this tell you? A revenue stream Dealers are unaware of?


With this data our experts will gather a Tru-Review that is tailored to your dealership. We will refer you to our legal resources and assist you with collecting any monies that is due from the past.
