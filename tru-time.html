What is Tru-Time?

ABOUT TRU TIME

Dealer Chargeback Recovery is a Real-Time Software & Recovery Service that helps dealers keep & reclaim their hard earned money. Our Historical Process can recover chargeback errors up to the maximum limitations set by the state.


We understand that there are so many moving parts to running and owning a dealership. You rely on hiring the best people and software to run your business. But the simple fact is that while these people are processing paperwork errors are made.


What we have discovered in the past five years while running dealerships and keeping a keen eye on the bottom line. We have been closely monitoring chargebacks at multiple dealerships have seen a industry wide consistent inaccuracy’s.


We have developed software to identify and flag these inaccuracies.


Even some of the savviest & buttoned up dealers that we work with don’t know that a lot of the chargebacks they suffer are due to errors on the party of third parties. Vin Software often causes false readings which produce dealer chargebacks erroneously.


The Dealer Chargeback Recovery Program comes to the rescue by discovering these false flags to prove the chargebacks are invalid. Once these cases are isolated, we can work to recover the funds back to the dealer.

No Heavy Lifting

We do all the heavy lifting for you! We research and investigate all your past chargebacks on our dime – there is no upfront cost to you. Once we find the error, we recover the chargeback for the dealer and then participate on the chargeback recovered. There is absolutely no risk to the dealer.

Easy Process

Our processes include Real-Time processing and Historical Processing. We begin with the Real-Time processing and move into the Historical Process after we prove our model works. Our Historical Process can recover chargeback errors up to the maximum limitations set by the state.

It Gets Better

After we recover your chargebacks, we can annually maintain the account allowing you to focus on what you do best: selling! You will have peace of mind knowing that you have the best possible protection from any future inaccurate chargebacks.
