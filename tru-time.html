What is Tru-Time?

ABOUT

We’ve reviewed thousands of chargebacks, so we know the specific errors lenders make when instructing dealers to cancel their products due to repossessions and total losses. It’s because of this experience that we’ve been able to build innovative software that reviews each chargeback in Tru Time, along with the applicable federal and specific state laws to maximize your monthly chargeback savings!


We understand how busy your back-office can be, which is why we have streamlined the process to save them time and you money. You will have direct access to your very own software that will give you instant results with the maximum amount of savings. In some cases, we may request additional information from the lender prior to cancelling, just to ensure you are cancelling correctly and maximizing your savings.


Our software has many other benefits for you and your team. You willhave digital records (Tru-Record) of each chargeback so you can monitor your savings daily.


Wondering how this affects your relationship with your lenders? In the thousands of chargebacks we have processed, we have not had a single relationship questioned. As a matter of fact, once the lender realizes that you have DCS on your side, they start doing things properly.This has been a practice and profit center for lenders for years. DCS is now putting that money back into the dealers’ hands, where it belongs.


With one store we have seen savings of over $80,000 in a year on repossession and total losses alone.


With Tru-Time technology we can start saving you money immediately and collect all the data needed to initiate your Tru-Review collection process. Just imagine, hundreds of thousands per year back to your dealership!!

No Heavy Lifting

We do all the heavy lifting for you! We research and investigate all your past chargebacks on our dime – there is no upfront cost to you. Once we find the error, we recover the chargeback for the dealer and then participate on the chargeback recovered. There is absolutely no risk to the dealer.

Easy Process

Our processes include Real-Time processing and Historical Processing. We begin with the Real-Time processing and move into the Historical Process after we prove our model works. Our Historical Process can recover chargeback errors up to the maximum limitations set by the state.

It Gets Better

After we recover your chargebacks, we can annually maintain the account allowing you to focus on what you do best: selling! You will have peace of mind knowing that you have the best possible protection from any future inaccurate chargebacks.
