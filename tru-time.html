What is Tru-Time?

ABOUT

We’ve reviewed thousands of chargebacks, so we know the specific errors lenders make when instructing dealers to cancel their products due to repossessions and total losses. It’s because of this experience that we’ve been able to build this innovative and proprietary software that reviews each chargeback in TRU-TIME, along with the applicable regulations to maximize your monthly chargeback savings!


We understand how busy your back-office can be, which is why we have streamlined the process to save them time and you money. You will have direct access to your very own software that will give you instant results with the maximum amount of savings. In some cases, we may request additional information from the lender prior to cancelling, just to ensure you are cancelling correctly and maximizing your savings.


Our software has many other benefits for you and your team. You will have digital records (TRU-RECORD) of each chargeback so you can monitor your savings in TRU-TIME. No more waiting for a monthly report. Because, by then, it’s too late!


Wondering how this affects your relationship with your lenders? In the thousands of chargebacks we have processed, we have not had a single relationship questioned. As a matter of fact, once the lender realizes that you have DCS on your side, they start doing things properly. Let’s not forget, his has been a practice and profit centre for lenders for years. DCS is now putting that money back into the dealers’ hands, where it belongs.


With one store we have seen savings of over $80,000 in a year on repossession and total losses alone.


With TRU-TIME technology we can start saving you money immediately and collect all the data needed to initiate your TRU-REVIEW collection process. Just imagine, hundreds of thousands per year back to your dealership!!
